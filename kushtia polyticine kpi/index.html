About

This institute was established in the year of 1964 and running under the command of BTEB. This is one largest and government technical institute in Bangladesh. The area of the institute is around 20 acres, where has a large greenery campus, academic and administrative building, hall rooms, laboratory, a large pond, playground, garden and so on. In this institute, there are 2300 students and 66 academic staff in the present. Let’s know more about Kushtia Polytechnic Institute information and Kushtia Polytechnic Institute admission process. Subject and Department of Kushtia Polytechnic Institute The institute offers 4 years of Diploma engineering courses in 6 different technologies. Here given the list of the Subjects and department.
